Beyond the Front Door

Place Dr. Doormat at key locations throughout the home where bacteria, dust and microbes gather such as underneath the kitchen sink, under pet dishes, underneath your cat litter box or at the entrance of your doggie door.

Replacing your dog’s pillow with Dr. Doormat helps eliminate dog odors and the spread of pet dander.

Placing a mat outside the nursery or playroom offers extra protection since babies and toddlers are the most vulnerable to household dust. The antimicrobial formula can’t penetrate the skin and is safe for all family members.

For allergy and asthma sufferers, placing Dr. Doormat outside the bedroom or on the floor by the bed offers an additional line of defense against dust mites and other allergens. Throughout your home it’s nice to know that Dr. Doormat is working for you 24/7.
