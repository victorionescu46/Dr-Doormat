Orders are shipped, with only a few exceptions, within two to three days of theorder receipt date. If the item you selected is not in stock we will notify you and do our best to ship your order as soon as possible.


Dr. Doormat, Inc. uses Federal Express for all shipments. Our shipping/handlingfee is charged per shipping address. All shipping fees will vary according to yourzip code and can be obtained by using the shipping calculator located on the checkout page. The length of time for Economy and Ground delivery is determined by your shipping zone.


Smart Post - Economy Home Delivery: Approximately 5-10 business days.


Home Delivery Ground: 1-5 business days. Select Ground Service if you can ship to your work/business address and save.


Ground Service: This service is available if you are shipping to a business or office address, not home delivery. Approximately 1-7 business days.


Express Saver: Delivery will take 3 business days, no later than 7pm.


Two Day: Delivery will take 2 business days, no later than 7PM.


Standard Overnight: The package will be delivered by 4:30PM the next business day.


Economy International: 3-5 business days (Canada, Mexico, Puerto Rico)


International Priority: 1-3 business days (Canada, Mexico, Puerto Rico)

Return Policy


At Dr. Doormat customer satisfaction is very important to us and we will do our best to meet our customers expectations with all of our products. We will refund or exchange any new, unused and undamaged merchandise. The item must be returned in its original condition with its original packaging, collateral materials, and a Return Merchandising Authorization Number (RMA#). Merchandise must be returned within 10 days of the original purchase date.


Before returning an item, a Return Merchandise Authorization Number (RMA#) is required. Simply email us at returns@drdoormat.com include your original order # and your reason for return and we will give you an RMA# to ensure proper tracking of your return. The RMA# must be included with your package at the time of the return. All returns must be shipped to the following address.


Dr. Doormat, Inc

Attn: Customer Service

3255 Shawnee Industrial Way

Suite 100

Suwanee, GA 30024


If you should receive an item that is "defective on arrival", please contact us immediately so we can replace the item at our expense, or refund your purchase in full. See the ABOUT page to read the details of our warranty.


Price adjustments on previous purchases are not permitted. Dr. Doormat will assume the "ship to" costs on any special promotions that offer ("free shipping") only on purchases that are not returned.
